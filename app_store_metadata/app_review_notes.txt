WHAT THIS APP IS
══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
CONTENT SAFETY
══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Our AI assistant uses a ContentSafetyFilter that:
- Blocks 50+ forbidden phrases related to predictions
- Auto-rewrites borderline content to safe alternatives
- Logs all filter events for compliance monitoring

Example forbidden phrases that are blocked:
- "will happen" → replaced with "may unfold"
- "your future" → replaced with "your potential"
- "destiny" → replaced with "journey"
- "the stars predict" → replaced with "patterns suggest"
