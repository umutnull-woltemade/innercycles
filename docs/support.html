Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get my birth chart?

Enter your birth date, time, and location in the app. Your complete natal chart will be generated automatically with detailed interpretations.

What if I don't know my exact birth time?

You can use 12:00 PM as a default, but for the most accurate chart, try to find your birth certificate or ask family members.

How do I restore my premium subscription?

Go to Settings > Restore Purchases. Your subscription will be automatically restored if you're signed in with the same Apple ID.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through Apple. Go to iPhone Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions > Venus One > Cancel Subscription.

Are the horoscopes personalized?

Yes! Our horoscopes are calculated based on your complete birth chart, not just your sun sign.

How often are horoscopes updated?

Daily horoscopes are updated every day at midnight. Weekly, monthly, and yearly horoscopes are updated at the start of each period.
