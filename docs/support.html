Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start journaling?

Open InnerCycles and tap on "New Entry" to begin writing. You can use our guided prompts for inspiration or write freely about your day, thoughts, and feelings.

How does mood tracking work?

Each time you create a journal entry, you can tag your current mood and energy level. Over time, InnerCycles identifies patterns in your emotional well-being and presents them as personal insights.

How are insights generated?

InnerCycles analyzes patterns across your journal entries -- such as recurring themes, mood trends, and focus areas -- to surface meaningful observations. These insights are based on your own writing, not external data.

How often is content updated?

Daily reflection prompts are refreshed every day. Weekly digests and monthly themes are updated at the start of each period to keep your journaling experience fresh and engaging.

How do I restore my premium subscription?

Go to Settings > Restore Purchases. Your subscription will be automatically restored if you're signed in with the same Apple ID.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through Apple. Go to iPhone Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions > InnerCycles > Cancel Subscription.
