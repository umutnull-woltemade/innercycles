InnerCycles - AI Usage Policy

Last Updated: February 2026

Transparency Statement

At InnerCycles, we believe in transparency about how we use artificial intelligence in our application. This policy explains our AI practices.

How We Use AI

Some content in InnerCycles is generated or enhanced using artificial intelligence technology. We use AI to:

  • Create personalized journaling prompts and reflection themes
  • Generate daily wellness and mindfulness content
  • Provide varied and engaging self-reflection exercises
  • Enhance dream journal interpretations and pattern recognition
  • Surface personal insights from journal entry patterns

AI Content Labeling

When content is generated by AI, we include a disclosure such as:

"This content is generated with AI to support personal reflection."

Content Safety Measures

All AI-generated content passes through our safety filters to ensure:

  • No predictive or fortune-telling language
  • No medical, legal, or financial advice
  • No harmful or misleading claims
  • Appropriate tone for journaling and self-reflection purposes

Human Oversight

Our editorial team reviews and curates AI-generated content to maintain quality and ensure alignment with our content guidelines. We combine AI efficiency with human judgment to deliver meaningful journaling and reflection experiences.

Limitations of AI Content

Please understand that AI-generated content:

  • Is not personalized to your specific life circumstances
  • Should not be interpreted as professional advice of any kind
  • May contain general themes that don't apply to everyone
  • Is designed for self-reflection and personal exploration, not clinical guidance
