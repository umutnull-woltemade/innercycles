Venus One - Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2025

Introduction

Venus One ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Birth Information: Date, time, and location of birth for astrological calculations
  • Account Information: Email address (if you create an account)
  • Usage Data: App interactions, features used, and preferences
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, and app version

How We Use Your Information

  • Generate personalized horoscopes and birth charts
  • Provide compatibility analysis
  • Improve our services and user experience
  • Send notifications (if enabled)
  • Process subscriptions and purchases

Data Storage

Your birth information is stored securely on your device and in our cloud database (Supabase) to sync across devices. We use industry-standard encryption to protect your data.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • RevenueCat: Subscription management
  • Google AdMob: Advertising (free version only)
  • Supabase: Database and authentication
  • Firebase: Analytics and crash reporting

Advertising

The free version of Venus One displays advertisements through Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data to serve personalized ads. Premium subscribers enjoy an ad-free experience.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-out of personalized advertising
  • Export your data

Children's Privacy

Venus One is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
