Venus One - Editorial Policy

Last Updated: February 2025

Our Mission

Venus One is dedicated to providing thoughtful, culturally-informed content that supports personal reflection, self-awareness, and mindfulness. We believe in the power of symbolic thinking and archetypal exploration as tools for self-understanding.

Content Principles

1. Reflection, Not Prediction

All content is designed to prompt self-reflection and personal exploration. We do not claim to predict future events, outcomes, or certainties. Our content uses symbolic and archetypal themes as mirrors for self-understanding.

2. Cultural Appreciation

We draw from various cultural traditions including Western astrology, Eastern philosophies, numerology, and dream symbolism. We present these systems with respect for their cultural origins and historical context.

3. Psychological Framing

Our content incorporates psychological concepts such as archetypes (inspired by Jungian psychology), pattern recognition, and self-inquiry. We frame astrological and symbolic content through a lens of self-exploration rather than determinism.

4. Empowerment Over Dependence

We encourage users to use our content as one of many tools for self-understanding, not as a primary source of guidance. We emphasize personal agency and decision-making responsibility.

5. No Harm

We explicitly avoid content that could cause harm, including:

  • Medical advice or diagnosis
  • Financial or investment guidance
  • Legal advice
  • Relationship commands or ultimatums
  • Fear-based or anxiety-inducing content

Content Sources

  • Traditional Systems: Western and Vedic astrology, numerology, tarot symbolism
  • Psychological Frameworks: Jungian archetypes, dream analysis, personality psychology
  • Mindfulness Practices: Meditation, reflection prompts, journaling themes
  • AI-Enhanced Content: Clearly labeled, safety-filtered, human-reviewed

Editorial Standards

  1. All content undergoes safety filtering for prediction/fortune-telling language
  2. AI-generated content is reviewed by human editors
  3. User feedback is monitored and acted upon
  4. Content is regularly updated to maintain quality and relevance
