InnerCycles - Editorial Policy

Last Updated: February 2026

Our Mission

InnerCycles is dedicated to providing thoughtful, evidence-informed content that supports personal reflection, self-awareness, and mindfulness. We believe in the power of journaling, dream exploration, and emotional tracking as tools for self-understanding and personal growth.

Content Principles

1. Reflection, Not Prediction

All content is designed to prompt self-reflection and personal exploration. We do not claim to predict future events, outcomes, or certainties. Our content uses psychological frameworks and mindfulness practices as mirrors for self-understanding.

2. Evidence-Informed Approach

We draw from established psychological frameworks, mindfulness practices, dream analysis traditions, and journaling methodologies. We present these approaches with respect for their origins and grounding in research where applicable.

3. Psychological Framing

Our content incorporates psychological concepts such as pattern recognition, emotional intelligence, and self-inquiry. We frame all content through a lens of self-exploration and personal growth rather than determinism.

4. Empowerment Over Dependence

We encourage users to use our content as one of many tools for self-understanding, not as a primary source of guidance. We emphasize personal agency and decision-making responsibility.

5. No Harm

We explicitly avoid content that could cause harm, including:

  • Medical advice or diagnosis
  • Financial or investment guidance
  • Legal advice
  • Relationship commands or ultimatums
  • Fear-based or anxiety-inducing content

Content Sources

  • Psychological Frameworks: Jungian archetypes, dream analysis, emotional intelligence research, personality psychology
  • Mindfulness Practices: Meditation, reflection prompts, gratitude exercises, breathwork
  • Journaling Methodologies: Guided journaling, free writing, mood tracking, growth-oriented prompts
  • AI-Enhanced Content: Clearly labeled, safety-filtered, human-reviewed

Editorial Standards

  1. All content undergoes safety filtering to remove predictive or fortune-telling language
  2. AI-generated content is reviewed by human editors
  3. User feedback is monitored and acted upon
  4. Content is regularly updated to maintain quality and relevance
