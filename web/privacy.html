Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 11, 2026

Summary: We collect minimal data necessary to provide astrology services. Your birth data is stored locally on your device. We do not sell your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

Venus One collects the following information to provide our services:

  • Birth Data: Date, time, and location of birth for chart calculations. This data is stored locally on your device.
  • Account Information: If you create an account via Apple Sign-In, we receive your Apple ID identifier and optionally your name/email.
  • Usage Analytics: Anonymous usage statistics to improve the app experience (via Firebase Analytics).
  • Crash Reports: Anonymous crash data to fix bugs (via Firebase Crashlytics).

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Calculate and display your birth chart, horoscopes, and astrological content
  • Sync your preferences across devices (if signed in)
  • Improve app performance and fix issues
  • Send notifications (only if you opt-in)

3. Data Storage & Security

Your birth data is primarily stored locally on your device using encrypted storage. If you create an account, minimal profile data is stored securely on Supabase servers with industry-standard encryption.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Apple Sign-In: Authentication
  • RevenueCat: Subscription management
  • Firebase: Analytics and crash reporting
  • Supabase: Optional cloud sync
  • Google AdMob: Advertisements (free tier only)

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Opt-out of analytics tracking
  • Disable notifications
  • Request data export

6. Children's Privacy

Venus One is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Data Retention

Local data remains on your device until you delete the app. Account data is retained until you request deletion. Anonymous analytics data is retained for up to 14 months.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or via email.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

privacy@venusone.com
