Frequently Asked Questions

How do I calculate my birth chart?

Enter your birth date, exact time, and location in the Birth Chart section. The more accurate your birth time, the more precise your chart will be. If you don't know your exact time, you can use 12:00 PM as an approximation.

Is Venus One a fortune-telling app?

No. Venus One is for entertainment and self-reflection purposes only. We do not predict the future or make claims about destiny. Astrology is presented as a tool for personal insight, not as a predictive science.

How do I manage or cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through your Apple ID. Go to Settings → [Your Name] → Subscriptions on your iPhone to view, modify, or cancel your subscription.

How do I delete my account?

Go to Settings → Account → Delete Account in the app. This will permanently delete your account and all associated data. Local data on your device will be cleared when you delete the app.

Why is my birth chart different from other apps?

Venus One uses the Swiss Ephemeris, one of the most accurate astronomical calculation libraries. Differences may arise from timezone handling, house system selection (we default to Placidus), or the precision of your birth time.

Can I use Venus One offline?

Yes! Birth chart calculations work offline. Daily horoscopes and some content may require an internet connection to load initially, but are cached for offline viewing.

Is my data secure?

Your birth data is stored locally on your device using encrypted storage. We do not sell your personal information. See our Privacy Policy for details.
